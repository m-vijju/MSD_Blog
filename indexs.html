Famous Temple in Tamil Nadu-Adi Yogi

The Adiyogi Temple is a striking and spiritual landmark located near Coimbatore in the southern state of Tamil Nadu, India. It is part of the Isha Yoga Center, founded by Sadhguru Jaggi Vasudev, a renowned spiritual leader and yogi. The temple is dedicated to Adiyogi, which means the "first yogi," a representation of Lord Shiva who is considered the originator of yoga.

Famous Temple in Uttarakhand-Kedarnath

Kedarnath is a town located in the Indian state of Uttarakhand, nestled in the Himalayas. It is one of the most revered pilgrimage sites for Hindus, primarily known for the Kedarnath Temple, which is dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas (shrines where Shiva is worshipped in the form of a lingam of light) and holds immense spiritual significance
